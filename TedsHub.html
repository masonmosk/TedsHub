COME GROW WITH TED'S

Your path from Team Member all the way to District Manager.

Team Member

  • Delivers exceptional guest experiences
  • Demonstrates our values and traits
  • Maintains health & safety standards
  • Practices good communication

Certified Trainer

  • Mastered all Team Member skills
  • Trains new hires using the guide
  • Models Ted's values daily

Grill Master

  • Masters the 900° charcoal grill
  • Cooks to order with precision
  • Upholds quality standards

Shift Lead

  • Key holder position
  • Assists daily store operations
  • Monitors & updates inventory
  • Food Safety Manager Certified

Assistant GM

  • Drives KPIs through operations
  • Supports P&L management
  • Coaches team & shift leads

General Manager

  • Leads with passion for food quality
  • Strategizes sales growth
  • Builds ideal teams
  • Owns P&L and inventory
Area Manager

Oversees multiple store locations, drives KPI performance, and ensures brand consistency across the area. Area Managers are seasoned GMs who've proven they can lead teams to sustained excellence.

District Manager

Sets clear vision and strategy for the district. Ensures flawless execution of brand standards, monitors budgets and financial performance, and drives accountability across the team — all while fostering growth and success.

OUR VALUES

Clean, Comfortable & Authentic — what makes a Ted's a Ted's.

Ted's Story

Ted's is a Western New York institution that began in 1927 when our founder, Greek immigrant Theodore Spiro Liaros, started selling hot dogs from a horse-drawn cart near the Peace Bridge. The first permanent stand was a small tool shed — the beginning of a legacy.

What truly sets a Ted's hot dog apart is its preparation: cooked to order over real hardwood charcoal, giving it a signature snap and a smoky, charred flavor. This commitment to quality and unique flavor is what we uphold every single day.

Inspiring family connections with food and hospitality

We believe the choices we make in our restaurants have a powerful impact on the memories and traditions that families share forever.

Treating our trays like a canvas

Our team prides themselves as being Tray Artists. Each tray that leaves our frontline is a future photo for the world to see on Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok.

Clean, Comfortable & Authentic is what makes a Ted's a Ted's

Comfort comes from a clean space, a friendly hello, opening a door, or clearing a tray. Being clean and comfortable allows our guests to keep their traditions alive.

We cook like the pros

Our main engine is our Grill — a 900-degree cast iron grill with real flames and real hardwood lump charcoal. Perfect for flavor and speed, from hot dogs to steak sandwiches.
